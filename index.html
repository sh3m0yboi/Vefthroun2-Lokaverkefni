Um viðburðinn

Tónleikar sem Spotify heldur með nokkrum söngvurum sem buðu sig fram til að syngja þeirra bestu lög. Viðburðurðinn er 13, 14, 15 og 16 mars árið 2026. Það er ekki oft sem nokkrir söngvarar eru með tónleika í einu. Söngvararnir eru allt frá vinsælum til mjög vanmetinna. Spotify er sænsk streymis- og miðlaþjónusta sem var stofnuð í apríl 2006 af Daniel Ek og Martin Lorentzon. Í desember 2025 var hún ein af stærstu tónlistarstreymisþjónustum heims, með yfir 751 milljón mánaðarlega virka notendur, þar af 290 milljónir greiðandi áskrifenda. Spotify er skráð á Verðbréfaþingi New York í formi bandarískra vörsluskírteina (American depositary receipts).

Bryant Barnes

Bryant Barnes (born March 13, 2003), is an American singer-songwriter and musician. He originally gained virality on TikTok through piano covers, but later signed with Mercury Records and began to put out R&B music. He released his first extended play, Vanity in 2024. It contained the hit song "I'd Rather Pretend", which was later made into a remix featuring d4vd. His debut studio album, Solace, was released on October 17, 2025.

Laufey

Laufey Lín Bing Jónsdóttir (born 23 April 1999) known mononymously as Laufey is an Icelandic singer-songwriter and musician. Her musical style blends genres such as jazz pop, classical and bossa nova. Laufey began performing as a cello soloist with the Iceland Symphony Orchestra at age 15. She then emerged as a finalist in the 2014 edition of Ísland Got Talent (Iceland's Got Talent) and a semifinalist on The Voice Iceland in 2015.

Giveon

Giveon Dezmann Evans (born February 21, 1995), mononymously known as Giveon (stylized as GIVĒON), is an American R&B singer and songwriter. He rose to prominence with his 2019 song "Like I Want You", which peaked at number 87 on the Billboard Hot 100. In 2020, Giveon signed with Epic Records to release two extended plays: the Grammy Award-nominated Take Time and When It's All Said and Done; the former was preceded by the single "Heartbreak Anniversary", which peaked at number 16 on the chart and received quintuple platinum certification by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).

Midday Wednesday

Midday Wednesday is a five-piece alt-indie band from Quezon City, Philippines. The band is composed of Rian Mallorca (vocals, rhythm guitar), Gab Dungca (guitar), Vigo Lanzona (vocals, bass), Tristan Monsod (drums), and Joax Tanpoco (keyboard). Formed in the summer of 2019, the band creates music that reflects the beauty of individual nuances and human vulnerability.
